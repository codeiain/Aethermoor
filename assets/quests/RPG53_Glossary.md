# RPG53 Quest Glossary

- Watcher Stone: A carved monolith at the heart of the Whispering Forest. Inscription warns of threats beneath the roots and guides seekers to the Sunken Crypt.
- Watcher Stone (variant): Alternate spelling used in some texts; refer to Watcher Stone entry for canonical meaning.
- Moonpetals: Rare forest herbs used by healers; gathered from shaded clearings near the Thornwood Edge.
- Emperor's Path (The Empire): Ancient stone road linking settlements; central to Thornhaven lore.
- Hartwick family: Longtime Thornhaven farmers; the family saga ties to Thornhaven’s founding history.
- Ashroot Tower: Ruined watch-tower east of Thornhaven Fields; line of defense from the old Empire.
- Millhaven: Town at the edge of Whispering Forest; starting hub for early quests.
- Whispering Forest: A dense forest area between Millhaven and Sunken Crypt; home to goblins and thorn sprites.
- Sunken Crypt: Subterranean ruin accessed after quest 3; site of Rot Shaman activity.
- Rot Shaman: Antagonist mentioned as raising the dead in the crypt; early story hook.
- Rot Lord: Main story boss introduced later in the Rot Lord Falls arc.
- Thornhaven: Village hub with a militia and craftsmen; primary setting for Thornhaven Quests.
- Thornhaven Fields: Rural road area surrounding Thornhaven; goblin activity hotspot.
- Ashroot Tower: Old empire watch-post; contains inscriptions linking to the lore.
