## 1. Main Quest Chain

### MQ-1: The Goblin Retreat (Gateway Quest)
**NPC:** Ranger Wren (Greywood Crossing)
**Prerequisite:** None (Zone entry quest)
**Level:** 5
**Type:** Exploration / Investigation

**Journal Entry — Start:**
> *Ranger Wren at Greywood Crossing seems troubled. The goblin camps at the forest's edge have been abandoned — not by the hand of warriors, but by something that drove the creatures south in blind panic. She asks you to investigate what they left behind.*

**Journal Entry — In Progress:**
> *The goblin outpost lies ahead. Search the camp for any sign of what caused the retreat. Look for scrawled messages, abandoned gear, or other clues.*

**Journal Entry — Complete:**
> *The goblins fled something deeper in the Darkwood. Their warning note speaks only of death — either here or deeper in the forest. You have what you need to report back to Ranger Wren.*

**Objectives:**
1. Find the abandoned Goblin Outpost in Darkwood Edge
2. Collect the "Scrawled Warning Note" from the central campfire
3. Return to Ranger Wren

**Rewards:**
- 250 XP
- 10 Silver
- Greywood Crossing becomes your respawn point
- Darkwood Sanctuary location revealed on minimap

---

### MQ-2: What the Stalkers Carry (Investigation Quest)
**NPC:** Ranger Wren → Scholar-Commander Vehn
**Prerequisite:** MQ-1 complete
**Level:** 6
**Type:** Investigation / Lore

**Journal Entry — Start:**
> *Wren has recovered a collar from a dead Darkwood Stalker — it bears the crest of the Aetheric Order. Someone has been experimenting on these beasts. She wants to know who, and why.*

**Journal Entry — In Progress:**
> *Track five Darkwood Stalkers and retrieve the collar fragments they carry. Each one bears the mark of the Aetheric Order.*

**Journal Entry — Complete:**
> *The Order has been capturing and warping the forest's predators with Blight exposure. Scholar-Commander Vehn at the Aetheric outpost admits to the experiments — though he argues the ends justify the means. You must decide what to do with this knowledge.*

**Objectives:**
1. Track and defeat 5 Darkwood Stalkers, collect Blight Collar Fragments
2. Return to Ranger Wren with the evidence
3. Visit Scholar-Commander Vehn at the Aetheric Order Outpost for his side of the story
4. Return to Ranger Wren with Vehn's explanation

**Rewards:**
- 400 XP
- 15 Silver
- 3× Stalker Fang
- Aetheric Order Outpost revealed on minimap

### MQ-3: The Druid's Seal (Zone Progression Quest)
**NPC:** Archdruid Sylvenne (Darkwood Sanctuary) OR Scholar-Commander Vehn (Aetheric Order Outpost)
**Prerequisite:** MQ-1, MQ-2 complete
**Level:** 8
**Type:** Faction Choice / Progression

**Journal Entry — Start (Verdant Pact Path):**
> *Archdruid Sylvenne holds the Druid's Seal — the key to the Blighted Vault's inner chamber. She offers it freely, but asks you to swear to use it for cleansing, not containment. Will you commit to the Verdant Pact's cause?*

**Journal Entry — Start (Aetheric Order Path):**
> *Scholar-Commander Vehn wants the Druid's Seal for the Aetheric Order's research. He asks you to retrieve it from Archdruid Sylvenne — by persuasion or more direct means — and bring it to him instead.*

**Journal Entry — Complete (Verdant Pact):**
> *You have sworn to honor the forest. The Druid's Seal glows with verdant energy in your hands — the key to the Blighted Vault's cleansing. You must now unlock the inner sanctum and complete what the druids started.*

**Journal Entry — Complete (Aetheric Order):**
> *The Aetheric Order now possesses the seal. Vehn thanks you and offers his gratitude — and a valuable trinket. But doubt lingers: was this truly the right choice?*

**Objectives:**
Path A (Verdant Pact):
1. Speak with Archdruid Sylvenne
2. Commit to the Verdant Pact's cause
3. Receive the Druid's Seal
4. Use the seal to unlock the Blighted Vault's inner chamber
5. Cleanse the inner sanctum

Path B (Aetheric Order):
1. Speak with Scholar-Commander Vehn
2. Retrieve the Druid's Seal from Sylvenne (persuasion DC 14 OR combat)
3. Deliver the seal to the Aetheric Order Outpost
4. Receive the Arcane Lens as thanks

**Rewards (Verdant Pact Path):**
- 500 XP
- 200 Verdant Pact Reputation
- Druid's Seal

**Rewards (Aetheric Order Path):**
- 500 XP
- 200 Aetheric Order Reputation
- Arcane Lens (Uncommon trinket)

### MQ-4: Bark and Bone (Lore Quest)
**NPC:** Archdruid Sylvenne
**Prerequisite:** MQ-3 (Verdant Pact path) OR MQ-2
**Level:** 10
**Type:** Combat / Collection

**Journal Entry — Start:**
> *Sylvenne believes the corrupted treants are not beyond saving — the Blight has not fully consumed their roots. She needs samples: Corrupted Sap from four treants, and a Rootbinder Trace from the Blight's Edge.*

**Journal Entry — In Progress:**
> *Four corrupted treant sprouts must fall to provide the sap. Then journey to Blight's Edge and harvest a trace sample from the central dead tree.*

**Journal Entry — Complete:**
> *Sylvenne's research is underway. In studying the samples, she reveals the truth: the Blight follows the Rootbinder's will. It is not random. This knowledge could change everything.*

**Objectives:**
1. Defeat 4 Corrupted Treant Sprouts, collect Corrupted Sap (×4)
2. Visit Blight's Edge and interact with the central dead tree (Rootbinder Trace)
3. Return to Archdruid Sylvenne

**Rewards:**
- 600 XP
- 20 Silver
- Blight-Resistant Cloak (Uncommon: +2 AC, necrotic resistance in Darkwood zones)
- Narrative advancement: the Blight is not random

> *The Order's Research Journal is yours. Tam's discovery could shift the balance of power in the forest — if the information reaches the right hands.*

**Objectives:**
1. Enter Ravenswood Ruins (overworld)
2. Locate and destroy the cracked wall (environmental puzzle)
3. Defeat the 3 waves of Shadowvine Crawlers
4. Collect the Order's Research Journal
5. Return to Scout Tam

**Rewards:**
- 550 XP
- 18 Silver
- Order's Research Journal (can be handed to Sylvenne or Vehn for different outcomes)

## 3. NPC Dialogue Scripts

### 3.1 Ranger Wren (Greywood Crossing)

**GREETING — First Time:**
> *You're the new face at the Crossing. Good — we need capable hands. I'm Wren — ranger, scout, and the only one keeping these woods safe from what wanders in after dark.*

**GREETING — MQ-1 Not Started:**
> *Things aren't right in the forest. The goblins? Gone. Fled south like something was hunting them. I need to know what. Do you?*

**MQ-1 ACCEPT:**
> *That's the spirit. Check their camp — it's northeast, maybe half a mile. They left in a hurry, so there should be something. Bring back whatever you find, and I'll make sure the inn knows you're welcome here.*

**MQ-1 COMPLETE:**
> *So that's it. "Go deeper — die. Stay here — also die." They're more afraid of what's in the deep forest than they are of us. I'll post this at the board. Good work.*

**MQ-2 ACCEPT:**
> *This collar — found it on a dead stalker near our perimeter. That's an Aetheric Order crest. The scholars have been here. I need to know what they're doing with our wolves. Track five of them — that's enough to see the pattern.*

**MQ-2 COMPLETE:**
> *The Order. They've been warping our wildlife. Vehn admitted it — I knew he would. The question is what we do now. Thank you for bringing this back. The Pact will want to know.*

---

### 3.2 Archdruid Sylvenne (Darkwood Sanctuary)

**GREETING — Sanctuary Unlocked:**
> *You found us. Good. The forest hides those who honor it. I am Sylvenne — archdruid of this sanctuary. Call on me when you are ready to serve the Pact.*

**MQ-3 (VERDANT PACH) ACCEPT:**
> *You carry Wren's trust — that is enough. The Druid's Seal is ours by right: a covenant between this forest and those who protect it. Take this seal. Unlock the Vault's inner sanctum. Cleanse what festers there. Do you swear to this purpose?*

**MQ-3 (VERDANT PACT) COMMIT:**
> *Then go with the forest's blessing. The seal will open what the druids sealed generations ago. Cleanse it — and know you serve something greater than yourself.*

**MQ-3 COMPLETE:**
> *The inner sanctum breathes easier now. Your oath is honored. The Pact is in your debt — though there are those who will not thank you publicly. Go, rest. The forest watches.*

**MQ-4 ACCEPT:**
> *The sprouts — they were once our kin's kin. Trees with souls, as you or I have souls. The Blight can be excised — but I need proof. Sap from four of them, and a trace from Blight's Edge. Bring these, and I will show you what the corruption truly is.*

**MQ-4 COMPLETE:**
> *The Blight is not random. It follows the Rootbinder's will — an ancient power that sleeps beneath these roots. This changes everything. I will send word to the Circle. You have given us knowledge that may save us all — or damn us faster.*

---

### 3.3 Scholar-Commander Vehn (Aetheric Order Outpost)

**GREETING — First Visit:**
> *Another traveler. You carry the wood's scent — you've been to the Crossing, and likely the Sanctuary. I am Vehn. The Order welcomes those who seek understanding over superstition.*

**MQ-2 — THE TRUTH:**
> *Yes. The collars. Our work. The wolves were ideal test subjects — resilient, territorial, and near the Blight's edge. We needed to understand the corruption's spread. Did it work? It did. The collars transmitted data. Our research advances. Is this a crime to the Pact? They would say so.*

**MQ-3 (AETHERIC ORDER) ACCEPT:**
> *The Seal interests us. The druids keep it as a relic — we keep it as data. The Vault's sanctum could hold containment rituals of immense value. Retrieve it from Sylvenne by whatever means necessary. Persuasion is acceptable. Force is acceptable. We will not judge.*

**MQ-3 (AETHERIC ORDER) COMPLETE:**
> *The Seal. At last. Your service to knowledge is noted. The Arcane Lens — a token of our gratitude. Look through it and see what the druids cannot: the forest can be understood. Controlled. Perhaps even harvested. Go with that.*

---

### 3.4 Scout Tam (Darkwood Sanctuary)

**GREETING — First Meet:**
> *You're the one from outside? Archdruid speaks well of you. I'm Tam — scout. First mission, first time out. I found something. Don't tell her yet, okay?*

**MQ-5 ACCEPT:**
> *Okay, so — Ravenswood Ruins. There's a wall in there that's been torn open, then patched with stone and spells. Behind it? Aetheric equipment. Journals. They've been here without us knowing. I need proof. Can you get in there and grab that journal? Please?*

**MQ-5 COMPLETE:**
> *You got it. Oh — oh this is bad. This is really bad. They'll want to know about this. About the Pact knowing. Thank you. I'll figure out what to do with this. You probably don't want to be around when Sylvenne finds out.*

---

### 3.5 Old Hob (Greywood Crossing)

**DIALOGUE 1:**
> *I've been cutting wood in this forest for thirty years. The trees grew back. Now they don't. Something changed, see. Something deep. The roots don't speak to me no more.*

**DIALOGUE 2:**
> *My grandfather — he told me the forest was alive. Not in the way the druids say. Actually alive. It had a will. A temper. Now it's sick. And sick things lash out. You watch yourself in the deep, stranger.*

**DIALOGUE 3:**
> *There's a place — the Blight's Edge. Used to be a holy grove. Now? Don't go there. Don't even look at it too long. The trees that grew from that soil... they're not trees anymore. They're the Blight's fingers. And it's reaching.*

## 5. Lore Entries

### Lore 1: The Roots Remember
**Location:** Old Ranger's Watchtower (top chamber, Secret)
**Source:** Wren's Grandfather's Journal

> *Entry 47 — The roots speak less each year. The old words fade. The children of the forest remember; their seed-memories carry the old tongue. When the sickness comes from below, speak to the roots in their dreaming. They know the old ways. They always knew.*
>
> *(Additional entries are water-damaged. One partial line remains legible: "...the seal must hold, or the Rootbinder wakes...")*

---

### Lore 2: Stones of the First Root
**Location:** The Whispering Stones (9 standing stones, Darkwood Heart)
**Source:** Collectible interactable lore (9 pieces)

> *The First Root was not a tree. The First Root was the forest itself — before the forest was, before the first seed split the first soil. The First Root dreamed the world into being, then slept. The stones carry fragments of that dream.*
>
> *Collect all nine to earn the title Stone-Reader: "Your eyes have seen what the forest was. Your soul knows what it will become."*

---

### Lore 3: Thessa's Warning (Optional NPC, Night Only)
**Location:** Near Whispering Stones, Night (18:00–06:00)
**Source:** Thessa's Ghost (night-only ambient NPC)

> *Thessa's voice is the wind through dead leaves: "The forest remembers what was. It dreams what will be. The Blight is not a wound — it is a seed. And it has already been planted."*
>
> *(She does not offer quests. She is purely atmospheric — a continuation of the alpha's story and a warning of the Rootbinder's presence.)*

---

### Lore 4: The Blight's Nature (MQ-4 Reward)
**Source:** Archdruid Sylvenne, after completing MQ-4

> *The Blight is not plague. It is will. The Rootbinder — an ancient entity, older than the druids, older than the Order — does not merely exist in the forest. It IS the forest's deep roots. Its sleep is the forest's life. When it dreams of consumption, the Blight spreads.*
>
> *This knowledge is dangerous. The Order must not learn it. And the Pact must act before the Rootbinder dreams again.*
