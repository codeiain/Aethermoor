## Overview

This document provides the lore, NPC population, and enemy flavour for the three alpha seed zones:
**Millhaven**, **Whispering Forest**, and **Sunken Crypt B1**.
Zone descriptions are shown to players as a text overlay on zone entry (fade-in, 4 seconds, dismissable by tap).

---

## Zone 1 — Millhaven

### Zone Description (on entry)
> *Millhaven is a town that has known better days. The mill wheel still turns, the market stalls still open each morning, and the Tipped Flagon still serves warm ale — but worry lines the faces of its people. Goblin raids have grown bolder. Strange lights have been seen in the forest to the north. The townsfolk smile at strangers, but their eyes betray a question: are you the kind of person who helps, or the kind who passes through?*

| Name | Role | Location | Dialogue Hook |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Captain Aldric** | Guard Captain (Quest Giver) | Town gate — tile (12, 8) | *"I've no men to spare, but I've a task for someone with your bearing."* |
| **Sera** | Innkeeper (Quest Giver, Merchant) | Tipped Flagon Inn — tile (8, 14) | *"A room's three copper a night. Double if you want the one without the draughts."* |
| **Old Maren** | Lorekeeper (Quest Giver) | Millhaven Library — tile (15, 10) | *"The old writings speak of a seal beneath the forest. I've never had cause to worry — until now."* |
| **Gregor** | Blacksmith (Merchant) | Forge — tile (18, 9) | *"Best blades in three counties, and I'd say the same if they weren't mine. Prices are fair. Mostly."* |
| **Lila** | Child NPC (Quest Giver) | Market square — tile (10, 12) | *"Mister? My daddy went to get flowers but he's been gone since yesterday morning..."* |

### Enemy Types
- **Goblin Raider** — *"Lean and opportunistic, these goblins wear mismatched scraps of armour looted from travellers. They fight dirty and flee fast. They have been pushed out of the deep forest by something they refuse to name."*
- **Goblin Chief** (Elite) — *"Bigger than the rest, wearing a helmet three sizes too large. He carries himself with the confidence of a creature who has never been seriously challenged — until now."*

## Zone 2 — Whispering Forest

### Zone Description (on entry)
> *The Whispering Forest earned its name long before the trouble started. Locals say the wind through the ancient canopy sounds like voices — prayers, perhaps, from an age when people knew what lived beneath the roots. The trees grow close and tall here, and the light arrives in broken shards. Moss-covered stones mark paths that predate any road. Whatever the goblins are fleeing from, they are fleeing through here.*

### NPC Population

| Name | Role | Location | Dialogue Hook |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Willem** | Injured Farmer (Escort NPC — Quest 5) | Forest interior — tile (14, 9) | *"Oh, thank the stars. I went after heartblooms and turned my ankle on a cursed root. Can you get me home?"* |
| **Thessa** | Forest Hermit (Lorekeeper) | Hermit's Hut — tile (22, 15) | *"The crypt beneath this forest is old. Older than Millhaven, older than the kingdom. Whatever is waking down there — it was sealed for a reason."* |
| **Bram** | Lost Hunter (Side NPC) | Forest eastern trail — tile (28, 11) | *"I was tracking a deer when I heard something below the ground. A groan, like the earth itself was restless. I turned around and came straight back."* |

### Enemy Types
- **Goblin Scout** — *"Lighter and faster than the raiders, these goblins move in pairs, using the forest's shadows and low branches to stay hidden. They carry shortbows and retreat if outnumbered."*
- **Thorn Sprite** — *"Ancient guardians of the deep forest — or something pretending to be. These small fey creatures resemble bundles of animated thorns and bark. They attack anything that disturbs the old stones, and their bites carry a mild venom."*

## Zone 3 — Sunken Crypt B1

### Zone Description (on entry)
> *The Sunken Crypt was built before memory. Its entrance is a crack in the earth disguised by centuries of root growth and leaf fall — easy to miss, impossible to forget once found. Inside, the air is cold and still, and the walls are carved with warnings in a language only scholars like Maren can half-read. Torches have been lit recently. Someone — or something — has been here. The bones in the alcoves have not always been so restless.*

### NPC Population

| Name | Role | Location | Dialogue Hook |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Inscription** | Environmental storytelling (readable object) | Crypt entrance — tile (2, 2) | *"Do not disturb what rests here. The seal holds. The seal must hold."* |
| **Restless Spirit** | Passive NPC (lore ghost — non-hostile) | Crypt corridor — tile (8, 6) | *"We held the line here. We were not enough. Leave before you are not enough either."* |
| **Shaman's Altar** | Destructible object (Quest 4 objective) | Chamber 3 — tile (14, 18) | [Interact prompt: "A crude altar of bone and corrupted stone. Something foul emanates from it. You could destroy it."] |

### Enemy Types
- **Skeleton Archer** — *"These were once soldiers, interred in the crypt with their weapons as honour. Now they notch arrows with the mechanical patience of the dead, firing at anything warm. They do not speak, but their hollow sockets seem to track you with something that remembers what purpose felt like."*
- **Crawling Corpse** — *"Freshly raised by the Shaman's Altar, these shambling dead are not the dignified soldiers of old. They move slowly, frantically, as if surprised to find themselves moving at all. They are easy to outrun — less easy to feel good about."*
