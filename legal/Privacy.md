This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use the Service.

- Gameplay analytics and error reports.
- Communications (e.g., in-game chat) if applicable.

Data Sharing

Data Retention
- Data is retained as long as needed to provide the Service or for financial/tax/legal requirements.

Security
- We implement reasonable security measures to protect data.

International Transfers
- Data may be transferred to and processed in other jurisdictions. We take steps to protect data in transit and at rest.

Your Rights
- Access, correct, delete, or restrict your data where permitted by law; withdraw consent where applicable.

Children
- The Service is not directed to children under the applicable age and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

Changes to This Policy
- We may update this Privacy Policy. We will provide notice of material changes.

Contact
